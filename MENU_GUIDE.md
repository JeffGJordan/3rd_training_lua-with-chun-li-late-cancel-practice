# Late Cancel Trainer - Quick Menu Guide

## How to Enable

1. **Load the script** in FightCade
   - `3rd_training_lua-master/3rd_training.lua`

2. **Start a match** with Chun-Li as P1

3. **Press Start** to open the training menu

4. **Navigate to "Display" menu**

5. **Scroll down** to find **"Late Cancel Trainer (Chun-Li)"**

6. **Press LP** to check the box (enable)

7. **Press Start** to close the menu

8. **Press P1 MK** to start practicing!
